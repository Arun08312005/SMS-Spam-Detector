About SMS Spam Detector

Our Mission

At SMS Spam Detector, we're committed to protecting users from unwanted SMS spam messages. Our advanced machine learning system analyzes message content with 98% accuracy, helping you identify potential spam before it becomes a problem.

Founded in 2025, SMS Spam Detector began as a university project to combat the growing problem of SMS spam. Today, we serve thousands of users worldwide with our detection technology.

How It Works

1. Message Analysis

Our system examines message content, keywords, and patterns using natural language processing.

2. Machine Learning

Advanced algorithms compare the message against thousands of known spam patterns.

3. Instant Results

Get a clear spam/ham classification with confidence percentage in seconds.
